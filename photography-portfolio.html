Closed Odeon

The walkway that leads to the Odeon has been left empty. With the new covid-19 guidelines put in place, entertainment industries like Odeon are forced to shut.

Before and After Lockdown

The difference between Manchester during lockdown and after a few guidelines being lifted can seen clearly in these photographs

There were very few people going into the city centre, let allown Salford Quays. Now people are enjoying their night outs and going shopping in the city centre.

The Runner

Salford Quays was mostly empty during lockdown with a small amount of people going out to excersize by running, cycling or going out for walks.

Quayside Media City

Media city had most of shops closed and off limits, apart from Costa which was only open for takeaways.

Beer Garden Line

The line at the Beer gardens were appearing after pubs start to reopen. People could only drink outside.

Trams

More and more people start to appear on trams since more places are opening.
